Why be a member?

SSSRJ Spirograph Productions are now accepting members to join their squadron of epic math artists! We want YOU to part of this awesome company. So become a member now. SSSRJ members have large benefits over non-members. SSSRJ members each have assigned powerpoint slides in our SSSRJ Community Creations slideshow. Additionally, SSSRJ members get to e-mail their creations to our staff and if we like it, we might give you additional slides to add more of your AWESOME creations! We take pride in our members and make sure that no members are left out, get treated poorly, and are given equal privilages and rules.
